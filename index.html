Why now

The missing trust layer for public Wi‑Fi

Before Network Guardian, users guessed. Now they see a decisionable score before a single packet leaves the device.

Global attack surface

Billions of public hotspots behave differently across airports, hotels, transit, and cafes. One trust layer needs to see them all.

Spoofing on the rise

Rogue SSIDs and fake captive portals target travelers and remote teams. Pre-connection checks stop the join before damage.

Enterprise readiness

Scores are portable via SDK/API, so VPNs, telcos, and apps can steer users to safe networks automatically.
