How It Works

Network Guardian combines on-device diagnostics, a global trust graph, and intelligent AI scoring to power real-time network safety.

1. On-Device Diagnostics

Express & Detailed scans measure health signals without sending sensitive data off the device.

2. Reputation Graph

Distributed telemetry builds per-BSSID baselines, anomaly markers, and long-term trust scores.

3. AI Trust Engine

A transparent scoring model blends diagnostics, community coverage, and anomaly drift into an interpretable trust label.
